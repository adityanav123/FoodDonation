Why Donate?

By redirecting unspoiled food from landfill to our neighbors in need, individuals can support their local communities and reduce environmental impact. Donating food to people or organizations helps counter poverty, hunger and at the same time, it can improve harmony, friendliness, and trust among residents. It has been observed that generosity releases endorphins that generate feelings of calmness, peace, gratitude, and satisfaction which help in easing tension and stress. Thus, people who help the needy get the benefits in terms of stronger immune system, lower heart rate, increased energy, lesser pain, and lower blood pressure

Our Mission

Our main missions or ‘mottos’ of creating this web portal is to provide healthy food to vast amount of people, so as wastage of food is minimised and food is available to maximum amount of people, and we also HOPE to motivate others in this endeavour to help other people to make their lives better.

Freedom is meaningless if people cannot put food in their stomach.

Help the people in need.

We make a living by what we get. We make a life by what we give.

Who are we?

We are just a portal between the receiver and the donor for simpler communication during this pandemic. We help mobilise food to the areas where people cannot conjure up food reliably, we try to make sure that shortage of meals is never an obstacle for a future with better health , more education and better economic status.
